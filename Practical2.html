Healthcare Highlights

High AQI Effects on lungs

Even healthy individuals may feel fatigue,heaviness in the chest, or reduced stamina for few days.

During Diwali, the joy of celebration often comes at a hidden cost to our lungs. When we breathe polluted air, microscopic particles bypass the nose and throat and settle deep inside the bronchi and alveoli (air sacs) of the lungs. “The body responds as if fighting an infection — by producing mucus, swelling the airways and activating immune cells. This leads to coughing, throat irritation, burning sensation in the chest, and shortness of breath. For those with asthma, bronchitis, or allergies, symptoms can worsen dramatically,”
